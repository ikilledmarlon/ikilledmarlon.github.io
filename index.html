The Curriculum

10 Modules. One Complete System.

A deliberate, step-by-step framework moving from core market mechanics to precision execution and mechanical risk management — reshaping how you read structure, control exposure, and perform under live conditions.

01

Price Action Fundamentals

What is Price Action, basics of reading bars, market structure basics, and setting up your charts.

02

Primary Trend Identification

Higher highs & higher lows, trend lines and channels, the 20 EMA dynamic guide, primary vs secondary movements.

03

Break of Structure Mastery

Valid vs false BoS, major vs minor structure breaks, BoS in trending and range-bound markets.

04

Two-Legged Movements

Market psychology of two legs, ABC pullback patterns, second leg trap patterns, measured moves & projections.

05

Signs of Trend Strength

Gap openings & trend days, moving average gap bars, failed reversals as strength, micro channels & runaway trends.

06

Breakout Trading Strategies

Successful vs failed breakouts, breakout pullback entries, inside & outside bar breakouts, BoS confirmation trading.

07

Pullbacks & Re-Entries

High 1, High 2 patterns, EMA pullback trading, micro trend line breaks, when pullbacks become reversals.

08

Major Trend Reversals

Wedges & three-push patterns, double top/bottoms with BoS, climax reversals, and trading the transition.

09

Trading Ranges Within Trends

When primary trends pause, range structure & BoS, barb wire and tight ranges, transition back to trending.

10

Live Market Application

Complete analysis framework, entry criteria & risk management, live chart analysis, common mistakes to avoid.
